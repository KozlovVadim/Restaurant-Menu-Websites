What we do?
We provide you the satisfaction of trying out many different types of burgers.
Our Menu can provide a lot more information on what kind of burgers we have.
We mainly focus on Lunch, with good filling food that will not leave you hungry,
well hungry for more of our food of course.
How we came to be?
First, this started off as a hobby, our friends and family loved our burgers and
told us we should look to broaden our horizon. Thus, our little restaurant idea was born.
How we came up with our name?
I, the owner am originally from Britain. After my restaurant idea i decided to move
to New York and take out a lease in New York's restaurant district. My menu and decor
had nothing to do with Britain, so I wanted to have at least something little that reminded
of my homeland. Then I decided to put it in my name, Britain's got burgers!
